Junior Program

Eligibility

Open to all juniors (Members & Guest) below the age of 17 yrs.

Duration & timings

The coaching will be for charged per month and conducted on Weekends of 2 hours sessions in the Morning and 2 hours session in the afternoon. The students have to opt for the batches as per their convenience.

Timings: Saturdays, Sundays & Holidays from 7.00 am to 10.00 am and 2.00 pm to 4.00 pm.

Note: All Juniors have to report half an hour before the coaching class and stay half an hour after the coaching class for warm up exercises and loosening exercise. Training Skills During The Junior Development Programme :

  • Short Game
  • Long Game
  • Speciality Shots
  • Recovery Shots
  • Skill Challenges
  • Mental aspect of the game on the golf course
  • Fitness programme with assistance from GYM Instructors of KGA

Basic Rules, Upkeep of golf equipments, Strategy for tournament, Play Etiquette on the golf course and club house.

Interact with parents to update on the progress of their children

One Class per month on Nutritional requirements for children with an expert on Nutrition.

All juniors who are interested may register.

All the slots will be allotted by the coach.

HGA and KGA

Dear Member/Parent,

Hyderabad Golf Association is proud to extend its partnership with K&A Golf continuing into the second year for further development of our junior players. We at HGA are fully committed to promote and develop our junior’s so that they can truly achieve their full potential in the game of Golf and compete at the National and International stage. K&A Golf comprises of Karan Bindra, Anitya Chand, Indrajeet Bhalotia, Akhil Nayar and Deepinder Kullar, who have been managing the DLF Golf Academy since 1999; India’s best golf school. They have been guiding many promising Junior Golfers at the Academy, who have won several National & International Championships.

The focus areas, structure, costs and registration details are listed below for your perusal.

Focused Areas

  • Evaluations on Long Game / Short Game / Technique / Fitness / Equipment / Course Management.
  • Development plan and report for every junior made by K&A Golf.
  • Personal Diary for every junior, to be carried for all lessons.
  • Use of latest technology - V1 Professional Software / K VEST 3D Motion Capture Analysis / SAM Putt Lab Analysis for Putting / Launch Monitor.
  • Evaluation of performance in Skills Challenge and Tournaments.
  • Quarterly Junior Golf Tournaments & Prizes.
  • Club head speed assessment and how to increase speed & distance.

The program start date is scheduled for the 17th & 18th of January, 2015, and will take place once in a month for two days (Four Sessions of Four hours each).

"2015" HGA - K&A JUNIOR PROGRAMME STRUCTURE

Elite Squad (Open for all Elite players at HGA)

  • One group session of four hours with K&A representatives.
  • Four individual sessions of half hour each. One two and a half hour on course lesson in a four ball with HGA coaches.
  • Four fitness sessions a month by HGA fitness trainers.
  • Travel allowance support for IGU tournaments.
  • T-shirts and Caps for tournament play.
  • One fully paid two day trip to train at the DLF Golf Academy, Gurgaon.

HGA Advanced Squad

  • One group session of four hours with K&A representatives.
  • Four sessions in a group of two hours each.
  • One two and a half hour on course lesson in a four ball with HGA coaches.
  • Four fitness sessions a month by HGA fitness trainers.

HGA Intermediate 1 & 2 Squads

  • One group session of four hours with K&A representatives.
  • Two sessions in a group of two hours each.
  • One two and a half hour on course lesson in a four ball with HGA coaches.
  • General fitness routines by HGA coaching team.

Driving Range

  • The driving range shall remain open from 5.30 AM to 11.30 Am & 1 PM to 7.30 PM on all days.
  • No unauthorized persons shall be allowed to use the range.
